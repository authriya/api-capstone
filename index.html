Let the Rijksmuseum come to you

Plane tickets to Amsterdam are expensive. This app is free.

Use the app below to learn more about the objects in the Rijksmuseum collection. You can either search for an art piece, or be surprised with one. Either way, let's learn about some art.

The Rijksmuseum is a Dutch national museum in Amsterdam dedicated to arts and history. The collection of the Rijksmuseum consists of 1 million objects and is dedicated to arts, crafts, and history from the years 1200 to 2000. Around 8,000 objects are currently on display in the museum.
